Ratatouille

Description

Ratatouille is made of eggplant, zucchini, and tomatoes in a sauce of crushed tomatoes, herbs, garlic, onion, and carrots.

Ingredients

  • Eggplant
  • Zucchini
  • Tomatoes
  • Herbs
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Carrots

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Dice and combine the onion, garlic, and carrots into a saucepan and cook until tender.
  3. Slice eggplant and add.
